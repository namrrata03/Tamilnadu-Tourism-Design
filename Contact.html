Explore Historical Sites

Tamil Nadu has a rich history dating back thousands of years. Visit historical sites like the Madurai Nayakar Mahal, Tanjore Palace, and Fort St. George in Chennai to delve into our region's past.

Embark on a wild adventure

Explore the lush biodiversity of our state by venturing into acclaimed wildlife sanctuaries and national parks like Mudumalai, Annamalai (Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary), and Guindy National Park.

Foods to try

Dosai

Fermented rice and lentil crepe, served plain or stuffed, usually with chutney and sambar.

Pongal

Traditional savory dish made from rice and lentils, flavored with cumin, pepper, and ginger.

Filter Coffee

Strong, frothy coffee brewed with finely ground beans and served with milk.

About Us

Established in 1971, Tamil Nadu Tourism Development Corporation (TTDC) showcases the cultural heritage, architectural wonders, and natural beauty of the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India.
